Our Menu

Chicken

Explore the delightful array of chicken dishes on our menu. Start with our Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken, a succulent chicken breast marinated in fresh herbs and citrus, served with roasted vegetables and garlic mashed potatoes. For those who crave a bit of heat, our Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wings are perfect, offering crispy wings drenched in fiery buffalo sauce with a side of blue cheese dip. Our Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a creamy indulgence, featuring tender chicken sautéed with garlic and tossed in a rich Alfredo sauce over fettuccine. The Teriyaki Chicken Bowl offers a taste of the East, with grilled chicken glazed in savory teriyaki sauce over steamed rice and stir-fried vegetables. Our Chicken Caesar Salad is a refreshing choice, combining grilled chicken with crisp romaine, parmesan, croutons, and Caesar dressing. Try our BBQ Chicken Pizza, topped with smoky BBQ sauce, grilled chicken, red onions, cilantro, and a blend of cheeses. For a touch of spice and warmth, our Chicken Tikka Masala, with its tender chicken in a creamy tomato-based sauce, served with basmati rice and naan, is a must. Finally, savor the Southern Fried Chicken, crispy and golden with a blend of southern spices, accompanied by coleslaw and buttermilk biscuits. Each dish is crafted to highlight the versatility and deliciousness of chicken.

Beef

At our restaurant, savor the rich flavors of our exquisite beef dishes. Indulge in the classic Beef Wellington, featuring tender filet mignon wrapped in a delicate puff pastry with a savory mushroom duxelles. Our Slow-Cooked Beef Short Ribs are a must-try, braised to perfection in a red wine reduction and served with creamy mashed potatoes. For a touch of the exotic, the Korean BBQ Beef Bowl offers marinated beef strips grilled to a caramelized finish, paired with jasmine rice and pickled vegetables. The Steak Frites, a timeless favorite, presents a juicy ribeye steak served with crispy, golden fries and a side of our signature herb butter. Delight in our Beef Stroganoff, where succulent beef strips are sautéed with onions and mushrooms, then simmered in a rich, creamy sauce and served over egg noodles. Finally, our gourmet Beef Burger, crafted from premium ground beef, is topped with melted cheddar, crispy bacon, lettuce, and tomato, all nestled in a brioche bun and served with a side of sweet potato fries. Each dish is meticulously prepared to bring out the best in every cut of beef, ensuring a memorable dining experience.

Sushi

Discover the exquisite selection of sushi dishes on our menu, each crafted with precision and fresh ingredients. Delight in our Spicy Tuna Roll, featuring finely chopped tuna mixed with a zesty spice blend, wrapped in seaweed and rice. Our Dragon Roll is a visual and flavorful masterpiece, with eel, cucumber, and avocado, topped with thin slices of avocado and drizzled with a savory eel sauce. The California Roll offers a classic combination of crab meat, avocado, and cucumber, perfect for sushi newcomers and enthusiasts alike. Indulge in the Salmon Nigiri, with fresh, melt-in-your-mouth salmon laid over seasoned rice. The Rainbow Roll is a colorful array of flavors, showcasing a variety of fish like tuna, salmon, and shrimp atop a California roll base. Our Tempura Shrimp Roll adds a delightful crunch with crispy tempura shrimp, cucumber, and avocado, all rolled together and served with a side of spicy mayo. For a luxurious treat, the Toro Nigiri features the rich, buttery taste of fatty tuna on a bed of rice. Each sushi dish is a harmonious blend of taste and texture, promising an unforgettable dining experience.
