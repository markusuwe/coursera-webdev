Our Menu

Chicken
Explore the delightful array of chicken dishes on our menu. Start with our Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken, a succulent chicken breast marinated in fresh herbs and citrus, served with roasted vegetables and garlic mashed potatoes. For those who crave a bit of heat, our Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wings are perfect, offering crispy wings drenched in fiery buffalo sauce with a side of blue cheese dip.

Beef
At our restaurant, savor the rich flavors of our exquisite beef dishes. Indulge in the classic Beef Wellington, featuring tender filet mignon wrapped in a delicate puff pastry with a savory mushroom duxelles. Our Slow-Cooked Beef Short Ribs are a must-try, braised to perfection in a red wine reduction and served with creamy mashed potatoes.

Sushi
Discover the exquisite selection of sushi dishes on our menu, each crafted with precision and fresh ingredients. Delight in our Spicy Tuna Roll, featuring finely chopped tuna mixed with a zesty spice blend, wrapped in seaweed and rice. Our Dragon Roll is a visual and flavorful masterpiece, with eel, cucumber, and avocado, topped with thin slices of avocado and drizzled with a savory eel sauce.
